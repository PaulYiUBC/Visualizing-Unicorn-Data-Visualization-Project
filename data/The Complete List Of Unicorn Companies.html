What is a unicorn company?

A unicorn company, or unicorn startup, is a private company with a valuation over $1 billion. As of March 2023, there are over 1,200 unicorns around the world. Popular former unicorns include Airbnb, Facebook and Google. Variants include a decacorn, valued at over $10 billion, and a hectocorn, valued at over $100 billion.
